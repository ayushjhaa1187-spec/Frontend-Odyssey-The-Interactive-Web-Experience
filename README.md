📖 Official Project Narrative
Every developer has a "Dream" phase—the spark of an idea that feels like floating in a nebula of pure potential. But that dream eventually leads to the "Loop," the reality of bugs, coffee-fueled debugging sessions, and the recursive nature of learning. This experience isn't just a portfolio; it's a living metadata-driven story of that journey.

✨ What Makes This Special?
- Immersive Storytelling: Not just a landing page, but a narrative arc that moves from the "Dream" of creation to the "Eureka" moment of discovery.
- AI-Driven Personalization: The experience adapts to your "Developer Persona" (Alex Chen) and reacts to your interaction choices.
- Tactile Interactions: Use keyboard shortcuts (1-9) to navigate timelines, or PageUp/Down to drift through the developer's consciousness.
- WCAG AAA Excellence: Accessibility isn't an afterthought. With a 7:1 contrast ratio, high-visibility themes, and ARIA-narrated transitions, the Odyssey is for everyone.

Technical Excellence
- Atomic Design: Built with modular React components optimized for performance.
- Zen Mode: A minimalist, narrated environment for those who want to focus purely on the story.
- Persistence: Your journey state and preferences (Caffeine Level, Zen Mode, Narrative Path) are saved locally.

Key Features
- 🎙️ Optional AI Narrator: A smooth, synthesized voice that guides you through the developer's inner thoughts.
- 🎨 Dynamic Aura Backgrounds: Generative backgrounds that shift colors based on the emotional state of the current section.
- 🐞 Physics-Based Bug Game: A literal "bug hunt" in the Growth section to fix physics-enabled glitches.
- ⏳ Timeline Scrubbing: Fast-forward or rewind the developer's career using a tactile scroll experience.

🛠️ Tech Stack
- Frontend: React 18, Vite 6 (Rolldown Optimized)
- Animation: GSAP (GreenSock) for high-performance timeline orchestration.
- Physics: Matter.js/Canvas for tactile, real-world interactions.
- Typography: Outfit & JetBrains Mono for that "high-tech developer" feel.
